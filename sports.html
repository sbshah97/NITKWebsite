SPORTS IN NITK



NITK is not just a place filled with nerds. Extracurricular activites like sports and games are given equal importance. It has a full-fledged Physical Education & Sports Department with one Students’ Activity and Sports Officer (SAS Officer) and one Assistant Physical Director who provides and manages sports, games and physical fitness facilities.


Outdoor Sports

NITK has an international standard swimming pool with a diving arena, well maintained grounds of Football, Volleyball, Handball, Hockey, Kho-kho, Kabaddi and Cricket. It also has two basketball courts (both equipped with floodlight), a lawn tennis court and a 400 meter track surrounded by the pavilion.

Swimming Pool

The swimming pool provides relief from the scorching heat in the summer. Students can come and register at the facility in order to utilize it. Registration involves submitting a photocopy of his/her admission letter to get the registration form, and then getting an approval from the Health Care Centre for swimming. It is necessary to be in a proper swimming attire. Instructors and life guards are always present.

Football

One of the most popular games at NITK, every evening one can find atleast 6-8 groups playing football on the ground. Football and practice cones can be issued from the sports complex by giving one's ID card. Inter college and intra college tournaments are held every year. Beach football is organized during Incident and a flood-lit football tournament is held during Phoenix.

Basketball

There are two flood lit basketball courts which are in great condition. One of them is reserved for the college team, and the other one can be used by anyone at any time. There are two major competitions which take place during the year: "Slam Dunk", an inter college competition which takes place during Incident, and the other one is an inter year basketball tournament which takes place during Crrescendo.

Cricket

Cricket is a sport every Indian is crazy about. And NITK is no different. Every year, inter year and inter branch tournaments are held. Cricket bats, balls and stumps can be issued from the sports complex by submitting your ID card. Selections for the team are generally held in the 1st or 2nd semester.






Indoor Sports

Indoor facilities include a modern gymnasium (separate for girls and boys), a well equipped badminton court and a table tennis playing arena with international standard tables. Also, a new and modern sports complex is under construction with modern facilities and amenities.

Badminton

The badminton courts are always filled with students, from 6 in the morning to 8 in the evening. Shuttles can be issued from the complex. Inter class badminton tournaments are held too.

Table Tennis

Table Tennis balls can be issued from the complex. Inter class T.T tournaments are also held regularly.
